About Me

I build dynamic websites for businesses and love working with data.

By training, I am also a Mechanical Engineer (recently graduated with my bachelor of engineering from NTU, Singapore). My undergraduate career is also where I fell in love with design and discovered my calling for sustainability. Henceforth, I have set out on a mission to #MakeSustainabilityProfitableAgain.

My idea of a retirement plan is being able to return to University and teach the next gen the crucial lessons on the synergy of business and sustainability I didn't have access to. :)
